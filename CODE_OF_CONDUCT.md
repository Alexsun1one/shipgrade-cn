# Code Of Conduct

ShipGrade CN is for serious engineering collaboration. Be direct, evidence-based, and respectful.

## Expected

- Ground claims in files, commands, logs, screenshots, or evals.
- Critique artifacts and reasoning, not people.
- Preserve source/license/security boundaries.
- Help Chinese beginners without weakening professional standards.

## Not Accepted

- Harassment or personal abuse.
- Secret leakage, prompt leakage, or private-source dumping.
- Fake benchmarks, fake adoption claims, or unverifiable quality claims.
- Contributions that make the package look stronger while making it less safe.
